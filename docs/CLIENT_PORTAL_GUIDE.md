# Client Portal Guide

The **Sovryx OS Client Portal** is a secure, branded area where your customers can log in to view their projects, pay invoices, and request support. This guide explains what the client experiences.

---

## 🔐 1. Accessing the Portal

- When a Client is created in the CRM, the system automatically emails them a welcome link to set up their password.
- Clients log in at `https://app.yourdomain.com/client-login`.
- The portal is branded with your company's logo and colors.

---

## 📊 2. The Client Dashboard

Upon logging in, clients see a high-level overview:
- **Outstanding Balance:** Total amount due across all unpaid invoices.
- **Active Projects:** Number of ongoing projects.
- **Recent Activity:** Latest invoices generated, support tickets answered, etc.

---

## 💳 3. Billing & Invoices

Clients have full transparency over their financials.

- **View Invoices:** Clients can view a list of all Paid, Unpaid, and Overdue invoices.
- **Download PDF:** They can download official PDF copies for their own accounting.
- **Make Payments:** If you have integrated a payment gateway (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay), clients will see a **Pay Now** button on unpaid invoices to settle balances instantly.
- **Approve Quotations:** Clients can review pending proposals/quotations and click **Approve** or **Decline** (with a reason).

---

## 🚀 4. Project Tracking

Clients can track the progress of their purchased services without needing to email you for updates.

- **Milestones:** They can view high-level project milestones and completion percentages.
- **Tasks (Optional):** If configured in settings, clients can view specific tasks and see what the team is currently working on.
- **Shared Documents:** Clients can securely download files you have shared with them (e.g., finalized logo files, signed contracts).

---

## 🎫 5. Customer Support Desk

Instead of scattering communication across emails and WhatsApp, clients can use the built-in Help Desk.

- **Open a Ticket:** Clients can submit a new support request (e.g., "Website is down", "Need an email account created").
- **Ticket Tracking:** They can view the status of their ticket (*Open, In Progress, Closed*).
- **Replies:** Clients can reply to tickets and upload screenshot attachments directly within the portal.

---

## 🌐 6. Services & Subscriptions (IT Ops)

If they are purchasing IT services:
- **Domains & Hosting:** Clients can view a list of their registered domains, hosting packages, and exact expiry dates.
- **Renewals:** They can request renewals directly from this dashboard.
