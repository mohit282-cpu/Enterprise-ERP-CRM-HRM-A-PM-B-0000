# Employee Portal Guide

The **Sovryx OS Employee Portal** (HRM Module) is a dedicated workspace for your staff. It allows them to manage their daily attendance, track assigned tasks, and access payroll information.

---

## ⏰ 1. Attendance & Time Tracking

### Clocking In / Out
- Upon logging in, the employee's dashboard features a prominent **Clock In** button.
- Clicking this logs their start time. The system captures the timestamp and, optionally, their IP address.
- At the end of the shift, they must click **Clock Out**.
- The system automatically calculates total hours worked and flags late arrivals based on their assigned Shift configuration.

### Task Time Tracking
For project-based employees (like developers or designers):
- They navigate to **My Tasks**.
- When starting work on a specific task, they click **Start Timer**.
- When pausing or finishing, they click **Stop Timer**.
- These logs are saved in Timesheets, which management uses to track project profitability and bill clients.

---

## 🌴 2. Leave Management

Employees can request time off without needing to send emails to HR.

1. Navigate to **HRM > My Leaves**.
2. Click **Request Leave**.
3. Select the Leave Type (e.g., *Sick Leave, Annual Leave, Unpaid Leave*).
4. Select the Date Range and provide a Reason.
5. Submit the request. 
6. The status will show as `Pending` until an HR Manager or Admin approves or rejects it. The employee receives a system notification upon decision.

---

## 💵 3. Payroll & Payslips

Employees have secure, self-service access to their financial records.

- Navigate to **HRM > My Payslips**.
- Employees can view a historical list of all processed salaries.
- They can click **View/Download PDF** to get official payslips containing details on Base Salary, Allowances, Tax Deductions, and Net Pay.

---

## 📂 4. Internal Resources

### Employee Directory
- Access a searchable list of all company employees, their departments, and internal contact information (Email/Extension).

### Knowledge Base / Company Wiki
- Access internal documentation, company policies, holiday calendars, and training materials provided by HR.
- This reduces onboarding time for new hires.

---

## 🎫 5. Internal Support Desk (IT/HR Tickets)

Employees can raise internal tickets to specific departments.
- Need a new mouse? Raise a ticket to the **IT Department**.
- Have a question about tax deductions? Raise a ticket to the **HR Department**.
- This keeps internal requests organized and out of standard email inboxes.
